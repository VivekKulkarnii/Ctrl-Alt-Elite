RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Residential Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 1, 2025, between 
Prestige Properties Pvt. Ltd. ("Landlord") and Ravi Kumar ("Tenant").

PROPERTY: The Landlord agrees to lease the property located at Flat 4B, Prestige Towers, 
Koramangala, Bengaluru - 560034 ("Premises") to the Tenant.

1. LEASE TERM
This Agreement shall commence on March 1, 2025, and shall terminate on February 28, 2026 
(12 months). After the initial term, this lease will automatically renew for successive 
12-month periods unless either party provides written notice of termination at least 
90 days before the end of any term.

2. RENT
Tenant agrees to pay rent of Rs. 35,000 per month, due on the 1st of each month. 
If rent is not received by the 5th, a late fee of Rs. 2,000 per day will be charged. 
Landlord reserves the right to increase rent by up to 15% annually with 30 days notice.

3. SECURITY DEPOSIT
Tenant shall pay a security deposit of Rs. 2,10,000 (6 months rent). This deposit may 
be used by the Landlord for any damages, unpaid rent, or cleaning costs at the 
Landlord's sole discretion. The deposit will be returned within 90 days of vacating, 
with no interest.

4. MAINTENANCE
Tenant is responsible for ALL maintenance and repairs under Rs. 10,000. Tenant must 
report any maintenance issues within 24 hours of discovery or shall bear full 
responsibility for consequential damage.

5. EARLY TERMINATION
If Tenant wishes to terminate this lease before the end of the term, Tenant must:
a) Provide 90 days written notice
b) Pay a termination fee equal to 3 months rent (Rs. 1,05,000)
c) Forfeit the entire security deposit

6. RESTRICTIONS
Tenant shall not: sublet the property; keep pets of any kind; make any alterations; 
conduct any business; have more than 2 guests staying overnight for more than 3 consecutive days 
without written Landlord approval.

7. RIGHT OF ENTRY
Landlord or Landlord's agents may enter the Premises at any time with or without notice 
for inspection, maintenance, or to show the property to prospective tenants.

8. UTILITIES
Tenant is responsible for all utilities including electricity, water, internet, and 
maintenance society charges which are currently Rs. 4,500/month but subject to change.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any disputes shall be resolved by arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 
1996. The venue shall be Delhi, regardless of where the property is located. 
Tenant waives right to approach consumer courts.

10. GOVERNING LAW
This agreement is governed by the laws of the state of Maharashtra.
